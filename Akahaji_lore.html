Prototype AI

Designation: AS-01

Assigned Developer: E.M.

Experiment henceforth will be referred to as AS-01. AS-01 is an AI develped by Zcorp. Created in order to simulate the [Metamorphisis] skill demonstrated by subject V-001. Neural net was derived from an incomplete brain scan taken. Aforementioned scan was interupted during a raid conducted by V-001's consorts. Despite the interuption almost 74 percent of the subjects brain was cloned. Development logs attatched
